About Commute List

This page exists to help people choose their best daily commute routes to avoid traffic jams. I'm sure that you'we been in situation when there are just to many traffic jams on your way to or from work. So to avoid that, you can help others on your daily commute to submit your best route, so that other can see when you're off to work, where do you drive and how much time you spent on the road.

Commute List uses Google Maps to calculate route and to show route on map. You can however modify your route by dragging route line (just like on Google Maps) to your wishes. Alternative routes are often faster than the one that Google Maps suggests.
